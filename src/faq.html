Frequently asked questions

And other tips and tricks to help you when you need it

  1. How do I store spices to keep them fresh?
    Store spices in a cool, dark place away from heat and moisture. Avoid keeping them near the stove or in direct sunlight.
  2. Can I substitute fresh spices for dried ones?
    Yes, you can. But, the conversion is not always one-to-one. As a general rule, use three times the amount of fresh herbs as you would dried herbs in a recipe.
  3. What are some basic spices to start with for beginners?
    Some basic spices to start with include salt, black pepper, garlic, onion, paprika, and cumin. These are versatile and can be used in a variety of dishes.
  4. How can I tell if my spices are still good?
    Check the color, aroma, and taste of your spices. If they have faded in color, lost their aroma, or taste dull, itâ€™s time to replace them.
  5. How long do spices last?
    Most spices last 1-3 years if stored properly. Ground spices lose their flavor more quickly than whole spices.
  6. How can I create my own spice blends at home?
    Combine different spices in varying proportions to create your own custom spice blends. Start with small batches and adjust to taste.
